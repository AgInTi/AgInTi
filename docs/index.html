Control principle

Autonomy must be inspectable before it is trusted.

  • Explicit permission boundaries before sensitive tool or lab-adjacent actions.
  • Readable logs, snapshots, resumable sessions, and durable experimental context.
  • Model-provider flexibility for teams that need speed, privacy, cost control, or frontier reasoning.
